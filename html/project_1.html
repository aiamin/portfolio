Ingredients:

  • 1 box of macaroni
  • ¼ cup butter
  • ¼ cup flour
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ground black pepper
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 2 cups of shredded Cheddar cheese

How to make it:

  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil.
  2. To make the roux, start by melting butter in a saucepan over medium heat. Add flour, salt, and pepper ans stir until smooth.
  3. Add Cheese and Macaroni.
